About Us

Baguio Multicultural Institute

Baguio Multicultural Institute prepares your children for a better foundation at a lower investment. BMI is a private non-sectarian and non-profit institution, under the management of CO-AN Educational Foundation Inc., located conveniently at CooYeeSan Convention Center, Naguilian Road, Baguio City. We warmly welcome your children from Pre- Kinder to Junior High School. Just close to home, we can assure you of your child's safety and happy learning. Come and see us. We'll be waiting for you.

BMI Vision Mission and Core Values


VISION

To have happy honorable learned graduates of different ethnicity who will be future leaders in their chosen fields of expertise.


MISSION

1. To provide a suitable physical and spiritual environment for the learners.
2. To have comprehensive guidelines and policies that will help realize the vision statement.
3. To form teams of competent staff for both operations and academics.
4. To build up enough financial resources that will support the various activities of the school.


CORE VALUES

BENEVOLENCE. To promote goodwill, generosity, and kindness within the school and community. At BMI, we motivate all members of this institutionto engage in acts of kindness towards one another and these acts are recognized and rewarded. Our learners, teachers, and support staff must know by heart that when they are kind, they brighten someone’s day and bring out the best in themselves.


INTEGRITY. To make choices that show honesty and truthfulness, applying a strong sense of fairness, justice, respect, responsibility, and accountability in all actions. At BMI, it is shown by makingdecisions that favor honesty and fairness with the best interest of our learners in mind. This helps develop our learners’, teachers’, and support staff's character, and promote trust in our school community.


PERSEVERANCE. To work persistently and diligently by maintaining a steady, consistent effort and determination even when faced with adversities. At BMI, it is shown by treating challenging tasks as learning experiences instead of potential failures. Instilling in our learners’, teachers’, and support staff's minds that they can do anything and that no dream is out of reach.


EXCELLENCE. To surpass what is usual and achieve the highest possible quality of performance. All members of this institution have high expectations for everything we do concerning academic achievement andprofessionalism in all facets. While understanding that perfection may be unattainable, learners, teachers, and support staff are driven by the relentless pursuit of excellence.
