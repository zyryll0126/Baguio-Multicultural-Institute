Pre-Kindergarten (Age 4 years old on or before Oct. 31)

Learners' exploration and learning about materials, routines, the usefulness of language, gaining competence, and self-expression are established.

Kindergarten (Age 5 years old on or before Oct. 31)

Our curriculum is focused on school readiness. They are embedded with language and literacy and will participate in activities that will unlock their knowledge in Math, Science and Technology, and Social Science.

Primary Education (Grade 1 to Grade 6)

School-aged learners are geared towards mastering knowledge and skills in writing, reading, arithmetic, and many more.

Primary Education (Grade 7 to Grade 10)

Offers an enriched version of the junior high school curriculum rolled out by the Philippine Department of Education. Providing learners the learning experiences that will equip them with the essential knowledge and skills necessary for success in higher educational pursuits and a wide range of careers.
